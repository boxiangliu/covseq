COVID-19 Genomic Sequence Database and Visualization

Introduction

CoV-Seq is a suite of tools and datasets for analysis and visualization of genomes of SARS-CoV-2, the virus that causes COVID-19. Sequences are collected from four major repositories: GISAID, NCBI, EMBL and CNGB. CoV-Seq hosts a growing list of full genomic sequences (66911 sequences as of 05 August, 2020), and derives genetic variants from these sequences. You can browse and download these variants and their annotations below (Data and Analysis). CoV-Seq also provides an interactive genome visualizer to upload your own sequences and to get analysis results immediately from the web server (Interactive Visualization).
